Passionate about blending technology and design to forge meaningful innovations and impact lives.

From childhood tinkering in my innovation center to understanding the nuances of user-centered design and technology in business, my journey has been a blend of creativity and practicality. I've seen firsthand how technology streamlines business processes, paving the way for more innovation. My ambition is to merge my expertise in technology, design, and strategy within the innovation consulting industry, empowering small businesses and product teams to create user-focused solutions poised for high growth and meaningful impact.

Projects & Entrepreneurship

Some design, engineering, and entrepreneurial projects that I'm proud of.

AppPioneer Camps

July 2023 - Current*
As Co-founder and Operations Lead of AppPioneer, the project I'm most proud of, I steered a tech-entrepreneurship summer camp that attracted 23 aspiring young minds. Through dedicated marketing and a curriculum combining design, business, coding, and interpersonal skills, we empowered students to develop and pitch their own apps, inspiring the next generation of innovators.
  • Company Website

Project Mia

Aug 2023 - Dec 2023
A semester long project done at Olin College explored the intersection of design and aging in society. My group and I partnered with Mia Melanson, spending weeks learning more about her and her values. After many prototypes, iteration, and further experimentation we landed on a design for a planner that organized her tasks and joyous tasks, forcing her to participate in joy every single day.

AimTrainer Plus

April 2023 - May 2023
In my software design class, I mastered key principles and the MVC (Model-View-Controller) format, culminating in the development of a comprehensive aim trainer. This application enables users to select a difficulty level and import their chosen photo, which is resized into a target. It tracks accuracy and score, offering a personalized and engaging experience.
