Project Mia

Aug. 2023 - Dec. 2023
A semester long project done at Olin College explored the intersection of design and aging in society. My group and I partnered with Mia Melanson, spending weeks learning more about her and her values. Denim Junction Sofa

June 2023 - July 2023
Guided by Parsons School of Design professors, this two-week project involved rapid prototyping, field research, and peer review, culminating in the creation of the DJF—a tribute to New York's bodega culture. I was inspired by the comfort that Bodega's provided to each New York, along with the lack of organization that these Bodega's had despite. Through this project I dove deep into the design principles of chairs and sofas, learning what makes both a comfortable and aesthetically pleasing seat.

AimTrainer Plus

April 2023 - May 2023
In my software design class, I mastered key principles and the MVC (Model-View-Controller) format, culminating in the development of a comprehensive aim trainer. This application enables users to select a difficulty level and import their chosen photo, which is resized into a target. It tracks accuracy and score, offering a personalized and engaging experience.

Crowd Sports

Nov. 2022 - Feb. 2023
My first design project focused on developing the UI/UX for a prototype of Crowd Sport. I conducted thorough market research through observations and interviews to optimize user flow and experience. Collaborating closely with the CEO, I wireframed and designed over 25 screens, ensuring the prototype's flow aligned with the envisioned concept. This culminated in a functional prototype currently featured in pitch decks, successfully attracting investor interest. Through this I was able to learn the importance of design and design principles that I carry through the rest of my projects
